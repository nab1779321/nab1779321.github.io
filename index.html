Na Bo

PhD Student in Biostatistics, University of Pittsburgh

I am a second year PhD student in biostatistics at University of Pittsburgh working with Dr. Ying Ding and Dr. Jong-Hyeon Jeong. I am also a graduate student researcher at UPitt. My research area lies in the development of statistical methodologies in personalized medicine. I am currently working on the development of a meta-learner framework to estimate heterogeneous treatment effect with time-to-event outcomes.
